## Description

### Short Description (80 characters max)
Professional weather forecasting and farming insights for modern agriculture

### Full Description

FarmCast delivers professional-grade weather intelligence designed specifically for farmers and agricultural operations.

KEY FEATURES:

Weather Intelligence
- Accurate 7-day forecasts with hourly breakdowns
- Real-time weather conditions and alerts
- Rainfall radar and precipitation tracking
- Wind speed and direction monitoring
- Temperature, humidity, and pressure data

Spray Advice
- Optimized spray windows based on weather conditions
- Delta-T calculations for ideal application timing
- Wind and temperature recommendations
- Automatic alerts for spray opportunities

Moisture Monitoring
- Connect to FieldClimate and other soil moisture probes
- Real-time soil moisture tracking at multiple depths
- Historical data and trend analysis
- Automatic weekly and daily reports via email

AI-Powered Recommendations
- Meet Farmer Joe, your AI farming assistant
- Get personalized advice based on your location
- Image analysis for crop and soil conditions
- Weather-based farming recommendations

Email Notifications
- Daily forecast emails at 7 AM
- Weekly moisture probe reports
- Custom location tracking
- Weather alerts and warnings

Premium Features
- Save unlimited locations
- Access to all moisture probe data
- Priority email support
- Advanced analytics and insights

Perfect for farmers, agronomists, farm managers, and agricultural consultants who need reliable weather data and actionable farming insights.
